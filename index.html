About Us

Traditional Masonry is a family owned and operated business specializing in custom stone craftsmanship since 1960/for over 60 years. Our unique skills come from a lifelong passion handed down through generations. Some of our specialties include walkways, steps, decks, fireplaces, retaining walls, stone facing, and custom details. We believe there is no other masonry company that can compete with our precision, integrity, experience and reputation for excellence.
